Chess NYC 2016 Fall Programs

Founded in 2007 Chess NYC has established itself as a leader in chess excellence and education. The Chess NYC philosophy is to emphasize sport and fun and provide as much chess instruction as a child can bear...but not overload...by providing supervised, spirited play, each student associates chess with fun, and therefore larger numbers of them ultimately excel or at least become proficient in the great, significant, game of chess...From there?... Excellence!
In creating an ongoing, vibrant "chess culture" where each player improves through camaraderie, inspiration and challenge, Chess Champions emerge with Chess NYC coaches (Read More) leading the way!

Looking to start a chess program? Chess NYC can help! Check out our Chess NYC Coaching Team
