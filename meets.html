Tournament Info

Tournament participation is an important part of a chess player's advancement and enjoyment of the game. An opportunity to play with "like skilled" opponents in a structured tournament is an experience that motivates improvement and creates even more cooperative students. From Head Start Juniorshort Sunday morning events (9:00 - 11:00 AM) with lot's of prizes! To USCF Weekly Rated, Sunday events (12-5 pm) for advancing players, up to the elite Saturday Quads for aspiring Masters, Chess NYC supports them all! The details and schedule are below...

Stress Free & Focused - "A more rational, supportive, productive and focused playing environment promotes a greater sporting, educational and enriching experience...We're grooming the very best Chess Players in NYC!"



Saturday: G & T Quads

10:00 AM (1000 - 2199 + USCF Rated)

3 Rounds | G70, D5 | Quads

Midtown West - PS 111, 440 West 53rd St. (E/C Train to 50th Street)

Every Saturday players will be paired in quads of 4, starting with the 4 highest registered players based on their last month published rating and continuing down to a minimum rating of 1000 USCF. Once your quad's rounds of games are complete, the next round will begin. Player may exit upon the completion of your quad matches.
